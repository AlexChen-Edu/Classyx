Privacy Policy

Classyx

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

1. Who We Are

Classyx ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered study platform that helps students learn through flashcards, study guides, and parent accountability tools.

Our website is located at getclassyx.com.

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:

Email: privacy@getclassyx.com

(Mailing address will be added once a P.O. Box is established.)

2. Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Parents who create accounts and manage their child's study profiles
  • Self-learners who create accounts and use the platform independently
  • Students who access the platform through a parent-generated access code
  • Visitors to our website and waitlist

Self-learners must be at least 13 years old or have parental consent where required by applicable law.

3. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

We may collect:

  • Email address when you join the waitlist or create an account
  • Password (stored securely and never in plain text)
  • Child profile information provided by a parent, such as first name and grade level
  • Educational content uploaded by a parent or user, including notes, photos, documents, and PDFs used to generate study materials

B. Information Generated Automatically

We may collect:

  • Study session information, including time spent studying, quiz scores, and accuracy rates
  • AI-generated flashcards, quizzes, and study guides
  • File processing information, including file type, size, upload status, and processing status
  • Basic technical information necessary to operate and secure the platform

C. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect:

  • Social Security numbers
  • Government-issued identification numbers
  • Precise geolocation data

We do not sell personal information or use advertising trackers.

Payment information is processed directly by Stripe and is not stored by Classyx.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Generate AI-powered flashcards, quizzes, and study guides
  • Provide study progress analytics and parent dashboard features
  • Send transactional communications such as verification emails, password resets, and waitlist confirmations
  • Improve, maintain, and secure the platform
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to customer support requests

We do not use your information for:

  • Targeted advertising
  • Selling data to third parties
  • Data broker activities

We do not use your uploaded educational content to train our own AI models.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable, we process personal information based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Providing the services requested by you
  • Performing our contractual obligations
  • Complying with legal requirements
  • Your consent, where required by law
  • Legitimate business interests, including security, fraud prevention, service improvement, and platform operations

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Classyx is designed to support parents and students while complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • Children under 13 do not create accounts directly.
  • Parents create and manage all child profiles.
  • Students may access the platform using parent-controlled access credentials.
  • Parents may upload educational materials on behalf of their child for the purpose of generating study resources.
  • Any information contained within uploaded educational materials is processed solely to provide the requested study services.
  • Parents maintain control over their child's information and may review, update, or delete child data at any time.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • If we learn that information has been collected from a child in a manner inconsistent with COPPA, we will take reasonable steps to delete the information promptly.

Uploaded educational materials are stored in private, access-controlled systems and are not shared except as necessary to provide the service described in this policy.

8. AI Processing

To generate flashcards, quizzes, summaries, and study guides, uploaded educational content may be processed by AI service providers.

Content shared with AI providers is used solely to provide the requested study features and improve the quality of the generated educational materials.

We do not intentionally use customer educational content to train our own AI models.

9. Payments

When paid subscriptions become available, payment processing will be handled by Stripe.

Classyx does not store full credit card numbers or payment card information.

All payment information is processed directly through Stripe's PCI-compliant systems and is subject to Stripe's privacy practices.

10. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide services and fulfill legal obligations.

Generally:

  • Account information is retained while an account remains active
  • Uploaded educational content is retained until deleted by the parent or account owner
  • Waitlist information is retained until removal is requested
  • Deleted accounts and associated personal information are generally removed within 30 days unless retention is required by law

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing information we hold about you
  • Correcting inaccurate information
  • Requesting deletion of personal information
  • Requesting information about how personal information is used
  • Opting out of the sale of personal information (Classyx does not sell personal information)
  • Protection from discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise any privacy rights, contact:

privacy@getclassyx.com

We will respond within the time period required by applicable law.

12. Account Deletion

Parents and account owners may request deletion of their account and associated information by:

  • Emailing privacy@getclassyx.com
  • Using any account deletion tools made available within the platform

Deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.

13. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information.

Security measures include:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access-controlled storage systems
  • Authentication and authorization controls
  • Database security policies designed to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact:

privacy@getclassyx.com

14. Data Breach Notification

If a security incident affects personal information, we will take reasonable steps to investigate the incident, mitigate potential harm, and provide notice where required by applicable law.

15. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use only limited cookies and similar technologies necessary to operate the platform.

Examples include:

  • Authentication cookies that keep users signed in
  • Security-related cookies used to prevent abuse and automated attacks
  • Cloudflare Turnstile functionality used to verify legitimate users

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party behavioral advertising trackers.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties and encourage users to review their privacy policies before providing information.
